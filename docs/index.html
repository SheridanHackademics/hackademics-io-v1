Who are we?

Hackademics aims to make hackathons more accessible to students, grow Sheridan’s presence in the tech community, win competitions, and give Sheridan students the tools and resources they need to build their careers.

The Hackademics Podcast

Listen to us chit-chat about hackathons from every perspective. We host students and professionals alike who chat about their experiences and thoughts on hackathons.

Hackville

Hackville is Sheridan College’s flagship hackathon. Hackville 2021 will bring together innovators, technology enthusiasts, designers, and entrepreneurs in a creative and collaborative environment.
